High Pressure Reciprocating Air Compressors 10-20 hp

Ingersoll Rand Type 30 Series high pressure air compressors feature base-mounted piston technology that provides pressure as high as 5,000 psig (345 barg). Typical applications include refueling stations, PBM, power plants and engine starting. Using the same durable construction found in all of our two-stage reciprocating compressors, the high pressure units offer reliability, long life and easy maintenance.

Features

  • Long-lasting: Durable cast iron frame and cylinder, rugged steel construction, NEMA rated motor housing.
  • Pump with superior efficiency: separately cast cylinders with deep radial fins for better cooling and efficiency.
  • Soft start: automatic start/stop regulation by pressure switch. The centrifugal discharge device ensures no-load starts for maximum protection.
  • Advanced safety features: Standard low oil level switch for constant compressor protection.
  • Reliable operation: All Season Select synthetic lubricant increases efficiency. With 2000 hours of operation between changes, it performs four times longer than petroleum-based lubricants.
  • Two-Year Warranty Available: Purchase an All Season Select Lubricant Start-Up Kit and receive all parts needed for start-up and the first year of maintenance, as well as an extended pump warranty.

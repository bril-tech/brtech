Non-Lubricated Reciprocating Air Compressor 7.5-20 HP

The Ingersoll Rand Type 30 non-lubricated reciprocating compressors, used in industries such as textiles, packaging and chemical, do not use oil in the cylinders and are designed for applications where oil-free air is required. The non-lubricated compressor is available in both receiver-mounted and base-mounted configurations to meet the needs of your application.

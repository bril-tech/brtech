Compressed Air Drying Units

Ingersoll Rand Refrigerant Air drying units are specially designed 3-in-1 heat exchanger which provides the very best combination of high efficiency and low pressure drop. The improved air-to-air heat exchanger performance reduces the refrigeration load and increases the reheat temperature. The resulting benefits are: smaller refrigeration compressor reduced power consumption and increased volumetric efficiency. The integrated stainless steel demister separator insures greater than 99.9% separator efficiency from zero to maximum rated airflow.

The advance controller has a full feature multifunction control panel with energy saving mode. A variable speed fan controlled from dryer controller automatically adjusts the cooling capacity to maintain a constant dew point.

  • High performance 3-in-1 Corrosion resistant exchanger.
  • Anti-freezing/energy savings mode shuts dryer off until dew point rises
  • Low pressure drop design
  • Variable speed fan for stable dew point control (in smaller range)
  • Full feature controls permit precise control in varying conditions
  • Remote connectivity/alarm/display/alarm history
  • Self-diagnostic software, anticipates possible faults option
  • Energy saving Condensate drain valve
  • Environmentally friendly refrigerant
